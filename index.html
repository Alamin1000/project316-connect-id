Connect Infrastructure Developments
(ConnectID) is a renewable energy
company that is passionate.

We are developing utility-scale solar
PV and battery storage (BESS)
projects in the UK.

#Connect Infrastructure Developments

At ConnectID, we understand that clean energy has never been more important than it is today. The UK is facing an energy crisis, and the country’s aim to reach net-zero emissions by 2050 is a significant challenge.

We believe that renewable energy is the key to overcoming these challenges, and we are committed to helping achieve these goals.

Beyond Solar

Our projects are not only environmentally friendly, but they are also socially responsible.

Where possible, we integrate agricultural practices into our solar arrays through ‘agrivoltaics,’ which allows us to use land in a sustainable way while still producing clean energy.

We are proud to provide impressive rates of biodiversity net gain on our project sites, demonstrating our commitment to the environment.

Our Structure

ConnectID was established in 2023 through a joint venture between Firstway Solar and Regener8 Power.

With over 50 years of professional experience in the renewable energy field, our team of experts has extensive knowledge and a deep understanding of the UK’s energy market.

We are proud to be a part of the solution to the UK’s energy challenges and are committed to creating a cleaner and more sustainable future for all.

Renewable Energy Solutions

Battery energy storage systems (BESS) and grid infrastructure solutions are vital components of the clean energy transition. These assets play a crucial role in balancing the supply of solar PV systems to the demand of the electricity grid, ensuring that clean energy can be used consistently despite its intermittent generation.

BESS systems are highly versatile and come with stacked revenue streams, which decreases financial risk as it allows for a more diversified income. These streams consist of payments for their contribution to grid capacity, performance, availability, and services. In addition to providing a reliable source of electricity, BESS systems also help stabilize the grid, reduce energy costs, and improve the efficiency of the power system.

As the UK transitions towards a greener future and increasing amounts of renewable energy are generated, battery storage and grid infrastructure solutions must work in tandem with renewable energy projects. This is essential to improving the UK grid's connection capacities and accommodating new renewable energy generation.

At ConnectID, we specialize in the development of industrial-scale solar PV sites as well as battery storage and grid infrastructure solutions.

We understand the importance of these systems in the clean energy transition and are committed to developing sustainable and innovative solutions that benefit the environment, the economy, and the community.
